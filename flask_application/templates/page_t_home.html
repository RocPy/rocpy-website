Rochester's Python User Group

RocPy is Rochester's local (and only!) Python user group. We exist to give others the opportunity to work and play with local Python developers, learners, employers, and enthusiasts. All skill levels are welcome, if you are interested in Python, join us!

We are just starting out, but we have a schedule of events (check out our "Upcoming Events" sidebar)!

  • Monthly presentation series: these are 45-60 minutes presentations on topics of interest to the Python community.
  • We also plan on having 5-10 minute lightning talks as well. Soon, we'll have a signup process for members wishing to participate!
  • Monthly weekend workshops are also planned.

In addition to meeting in person, check out our "Connecting With Us" sidebar to talk with your fellow Pythonistas!
